Tutorial

Take care of your pokemon for as long as possible. Their stats depletes over time, play with them to increase their mood, feed them when they're hungry, and make them sleep when they're out of energy.

  • Play: increases mood but lowers hunger and energy
  • Feed: increases hunger and a bit of energy
  • Sleep: increases energy and a bit of mood but lowers hunger

There's a 3 second cooldown each activities you do.

Each activities have three (3) outcomes: Good, Normal, and Bad which has equal chance of of happening. A bad outcome lowers the mood.


When a stat gets low, they can have some effects

  • If energy is 0, you can't play.
  • If mood is 0, the pokemon is bored and energy depletes faster.
  • If hunger is 0, the pokemon is hungry and mood depletes faster.
  • If mood is lower than 30, the pokemon will not be in mood to eat 80% of the time you feed it.

Don't do activities too much as the pokemon can over ate or over sleep which lowers the mood. They can't eat or sleep at 100 hunger or energy


Score is counted each second you take care of your pokemon, there's an additional 10 score for each activities you do, and 100 score if all the stats got 100 at the same time.
